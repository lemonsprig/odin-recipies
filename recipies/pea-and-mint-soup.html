Easy Pea and Mint Soup

Description

This delicious and super simple soup is the perfect meal when you want something quick and easy after a long day of work. With next to no prep it is ready to enjoy in no time.

Ingredients

  • 800g of frozen Peas
  • one medium brown/red onion
  • two cloves of garlic minced
  • olive oil 1 tablespoon
  • vegetable stock 1.2 ltr
  • one jar of mint sauce
  • grated parmesan (optional)

Steps

  1. dice the onion and gently fry in a large pot on a low heat in the olive oil until soft and translucent
  2. add the minced garlic and fry for anothe minute
  3. add 600g of peas and the mint sauce stir to mix, coating the peas
  4. add the 1ltr of stock and stir
  5. bring to the boil and simmer for 10 minutes
  6. with a stick blender, blend the soup
  7. add the remaining stock and peas
  8. return to the heat and cook for a further 5 minutes until the freshly added peas are cooked through
  9. serve with a generous sprinkling of parmesan if using
