Guacamole

Description

A delicious dip and powerhouse of goodness from mashed avocado brought to life with the zing of lime and fresh cilantro, tomatoes and onions. Add some finely chopped red chilly to add an extra bit of spice

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1/2 small red onion finely diced
  • 1 tomato diced
  • 3 tablespoons finely chopped cilantro
  • Juice of one lime

Steps

  1. Slice the avocado lengthwise and remove the pit
  2. Scoop the avocado flesh into a large bowl
  3. Mash the avocado to desired consistency
  4. add the remaining infredients and gently mix
