Greek Salad Dressing

Description

A simple vibrant and healthy salad dressign, perfect to brighten up any salad

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup of red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dijon mustard
  • 2 cloves of garlic minced
  • 3 tablespoons finely chopped cilantro
  • Juice of one lemon
  • 1/2 tsp dried organo
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 cup olive oil

Steps

  1. Add all the ingredients except the olive oil to a bowl
  2. Using a whisk, whisk all the ingredients
  3. Continue to whisk vigorously while slowly adding the olive oil
